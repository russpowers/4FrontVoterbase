Get Involved

So you want to help Vote For Marijuana legalize pot? Great! There are several ways that you can help:

  • Become a Vote For Marijuana member in your home state. Help us out by adding, analyzing, rating, and endorsing the politicians who will have the power to change state and federal law.
  • Donate! Vote For Marijuana has very high data and hosting costs.
  • On election day, print out a few hundred copies of the Vote For Marijuana endorsed ballot for your district, and hand them out at your local polling place. Everyone should know who are the drug warriors, and who are the good guys. (We're working on making a print-friendly page right now)
  • Help recruit more moderators for other states. In order to keep track of the thousands of state-level politicians in America, we need a small army of moderators. Do your part, and get your friends to kick in, too.
  • Share links to Vote For Marijuana on social media sites. We need more people learning about us, so please share us with your friends on Twitter, Facebook, and anywhere else you can help drive traffic to our efforts.

If you would like to become a moderator, please email us at [email protected] with your name, phone number, and zip code. We'll contact you shortly!
